Makurama

Gallery

Makurama is a collection of changing video essays inspired by Sei Shonagon: small lists, sharp weather, private pleasures, brief irritations, and the way attention keeps editing itself after a scene is gone.

The videos are recorded on an old iPod, edited, transformed to PAL, and finished with hardcoded subtitles. Their sound world is chiptune, human voice, and occasional ambient concrete sound.

This is open-source art. The website code, interface, documentation, and data schema are meant to be readable, forkable, and useful to other small web projects without requiring a build system or private platform.
